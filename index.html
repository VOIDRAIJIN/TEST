RIP_Indra: The Fallen God of Thunder

"In a world where gods once ruled the skies, there was one who wielded the power of the storm. His name was Indra, the God of Thunder and King of the Heavens. But now, he is known by another name… RIP_Indra."

"Betrayed by his own kin and cast out from the heavens, Indra's power was sealed away. Stripped of his godhood, he became mortal, walking among the very beings he once ruled over."

"But Indra was not one to be broken. In his mortal form, he roamed the earth, seeking a way to reclaim his lost divinity and take revenge on those who betrayed him."

"As the years passed, the legend of RIP_Indra grew. No longer the God of Thunder, he became something more—a force of nature, feared and revered by all."

"Now, his journey brings him back to the heavens. The final battle for his revenge and the reclaiming of his throne begins. This is the story of RIP_Indra, the Fallen God of Thunder."
